Highest High Points of the Class:

  • The textbook!!! The examples and content from the online textbook were such a great help.

  • The in-class labs were super helpful to identify what I understood easily and what I needed to spend more time working on

  • The projects were incredibly relevant and interesting and the partner aspect for p2 and p3 was very helpful

  • Being shown how to use the dev tools in the browser was awesome and helped me to get ideas about how to fix problems I ran into during the project development

Possible Changes for Future Classes:

  • I think it would be interesting to split the content up into frontend development and backend development, possibly by making kind of two teams out of the students based on which one they're more interested in and do the second and third projects with designated roles like that

  • With the idea above though, it would most likely require at least one additional professor/TA in order to effectively teach basically two things at once

Overall, this course was awesome and I think everything we did made sense and felt important to learning web development. Thank you for a great semester and I can't wait for 447 in the spring!
